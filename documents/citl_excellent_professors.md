# Illinois CS Places 28 Faculty on CITL List of Teachers Ranked as Excellent By Their Students

Source: Siebel School of Computing and Data Science (Illinois CS), 3/27/2023,
by Aaron Seidlitz. For the Fall 2022 semester, the Center for Innovation in
Teaching and Learning (CITL) ranked these UIUC CS faculty as excellent.

## Ranked "Outstanding" (the highest honor on the list)
- Jeff Erickson — CS 473: Algorithms
- Ryan Cunningham — CS 498: Law & Policy Issues in CS
- Timothy Chan — CS 598: Fine-Grained Algorithms
- Aishwarya Ganesan — CS 598: ML for Systems
